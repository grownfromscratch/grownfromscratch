A few tips for the best result

Go to chat.openai.com — a free account is all you need. You don't need ChatGPT Plus for this.
Paste the full prompt into a new conversation. Don't add anything else — just paste and press enter.
If you want to adjust the style of the illustration, you can add a follow-up message like "make it more watercolor" or "warmer tones" after the first image generates.
To save your portrait, right-click the image and choose "Save image as" — or use the download button that appears when you hover over it.
