About

Built by someone still
figuring it out —
and sharing what she learns.

I'm Genovie — based in Arizona — and like a lot of people, I entered adulthood knowing how to memorize information, but not necessarily how to build a life.

No one really taught us how to understand money, communicate well, regulate emotions, create healthy routines, think critically, or become intentional about who we were becoming. So I started learning.

Grown From Scratch is a collection of the things I'm exploring, questioning, testing, unlearning, and rebuilding in real time — across money, health, mind, spirit, and values.

Some of it is practical. Some of it is philosophical. Some of it is deeply personal. But all of it comes from the belief that growth doesn't have to be perfect to be meaningful.

Whether you're just starting out or starting over, this space is for people who want to live more thoughtfully, more intentionally, and more fully awake to their own lives.

"A fully developed human being doesn't happen by chance."
