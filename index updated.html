About

figure it all out herself.

I'm Genovie — based in Surprise, Arizona. Like a lot of us, I walked out of school knowing how to dissect a frog but not how to file my taxes, understand a lease, or process my own emotions.

Grown From Scratch is the resource I wish I'd had. Whether you're 22 and just starting out, or 42 and finally filling in gaps you didn't know you had — this space is for you.

"It's not your fault you weren't taught. But it is your turn to learn."
