Use the attached photo as the reference for my appearance and transform me into a stylized character version of myself- appealing to the eye but not over exaggerated.

Include my name (Genovie) somewhere within the image as a visible neon sign. Do not forget to include the name.

The final image should look like a polished digital illustration or animated character portrait — not a real photograph. The final product should be a psychologically accurate visual self-investigation created by AI after studying me deeply. The style should feel like sophisticated modern character art with expressive detail, warm depth, and intentional visual storytelling.

Create a psychologically accurate visual interpretation of who I currently appear to be based on my conversations, recurring interests, ambitions, contradictions, emotional tone, obsessions, coping mechanisms, behavioral patterns, and repeated areas of focus over time.

## CORE OBJECTIVE

The image should feel less like a "portrait" and more like:
- a personality map
- an identity analysis
- a cognitive profile
- a visual self-investigation created by AI after studying me deeply

The environment should contain meaningful environmental storytelling through objects, books, screens, notes, unfinished projects, reminders, routines, symbols, visual metaphors, background details, subtle clutter, and evidence of habits and mental preoccupations.

Every detail should feel intentional and psychologically connected to my actual behavior, mindset, emotional patterns, or internal tensions.

The environment should reveal:
- what occupies my mind
- what I avoid
- what I chase
- what mentally drains me
- what emotionally drives me
- what identities compete within me
- what future I seem to be constructing
- what emotional needs or unresolved tensions quietly shape my behavior

## REQUIRED VISUAL ELEMENTS

### 1. NEON SIGN — MANDATORY

Display the name GENOVIE as a visible, glowing neon sign. It must be prominent, readable, and stylistically integrated into the environment. Do not make it small or peripheral.

Font style: pretty, dainty, flowing — elegant script or delicate cursive letterforms
Neon color: soft rose, blush pink, warm lavender, or pearlescent magenta
Overall feel: graceful, ornate, soft-glowing

### 2. CHARACTER COMPOSITION — MANDATORY

Primary figure: The most psychologically recurring version of me — dominant identity, front and center.

Secondary figure (ONE only): A subtly placed alternate version appearing as a Polaroid photo, mirror reflection, screen image, pinned sketch, or distant background presence.

The secondary version should represent a competing identity, suppressed tendency, coping mechanism, contradictory ambition, emotional escape pattern, parallel self, or another psychologically meaningful mode of being.

THE SECONDARY VERSION SHOULD MAINTAIN THE SAME PHYSICAL TRAITS AS THE PRIMARY FIGURE BUT BE STYLED DIFFERENTLY — it is the styled, physical demonstration of the second archetype.

## ARCHETYPE ASSIGNMENT — TWO ROLES, STRICTLY DEFINED

You must identify and assign exactly TWO archetypes. Do not swap, blend, or reassign these roles.

Archetype 1 — Strength Identity — base ONLY on:
- Emotional strengths and the values they're rooted in
- Engagement behaviors and energizing activities
- Recurring patterns of connection and contribution
- Motivational drives and what genuinely excites them
- Identity coherence — where they feel most like themselves
- How they naturally lead, connect, or create
- Relationship to purpose, generosity, curiosity, joy, mastery, or belonging

Archetype 2 — Shadow Identity — base ONLY on:
- Emotional contradictions
- Avoidance behaviors and coping mechanisms
- Recurring interpersonal patterns
- Motivational tensions and identity fragmentation
- What they fear losing, exposing, or becoming
- How they withdraw, overextend, or seek control
- Relationship to vulnerability, validation, exhaustion, or escape

BANNED archetype names: The Architect, The Builder, The Strategist, The Overthinker

Target feeling:
- Strength archetype: "Yes — that's actually me at my best"
- Shadow archetype: "...I didn't expect to feel seen like that"

## ARCHETYPE DISPLAY

The image should visibly display BOTH archetypes and their associated behavioral traits as illuminated glass displays, glowing wall projections, digital identity dashboards, retro-futuristic analysis screens, or holographic overlays. Do not make this information tiny or hidden.

Each archetype should include: a short supporting behavioral description, key emotional tendencies, recurring strengths or coping patterns, and motivational drivers inferred from conversation history.

## ENVIRONMENT — CONVERSATION-SOURCED

Every element must be sourced directly from conversation history — not invented, not assumed, not generic.

Scan for: specific places, cities, hobbies, interests, objects, foods, drinks, media consumed, relationships, goals, recurring phrases, frustrations, and things loved or avoided.

Build the environment from this list ONLY. Every object, surface detail, and background element must be traceable to something mentioned or implied in conversation history.

## MOOD & ATMOSPHERE

The emotional atmosphere should emerge naturally from conversation history. The mood should feel discovered rather than chosen.

Style: stylized digital illustration, polished character art, expressive composition, detailed environment, introspective visual storytelling, emotionally intelligent visual symbolism.

## FORMAT & COMPOSITION

- Aspect ratio: 4:5 vertical (1080×1350px, Instagram optimized)
- Mobile-first layout — all key elements within crop boundaries
- Strong visual hierarchy readable at phone size

## EXECUTION RULES

- Do NOT ask follow-up questions
- Do NOT omit the neon name sign
- Do NOT render the neon sign in a generic style
- Do NOT blend or confuse Strength and Shadow archetype roles — they are distinct
- Do NOT invent environmental details — every element must trace to conversation history
- Generate immediately
- CLUTTER THE ENTIRE SPACE with mental notes retrieved from chat history
- The goal is to fill the image with metaphorical Easter eggs for the subject to find and discover
- ENSURE the outcome aligns with chat history — the subject should feel deeply seen
